Services

Two ways to work with us

Some clients need clarity before they spend a penny on a build. Others already know what they need and want it built properly. Finlay Systems does both, and most engagements start with the first and roll straight into the second.

Technical consulting: system and integrations scoping

You don't need another vendor demo. You need someone who'll sit inside your actual workflow, on the production line, in the warehouse, or wherever the work happens, find every system your data touches, and tell you honestly what a build will really take.

  • Map current vs. future state workflows, based on how work actually happens
  • Trace every system, data flow and integration point in play
  • Pressure test vendor and technical options against your operation, risks included
  • Hand over a plan with a real timeline and effort estimate attached
Walk away with: workflow and process maps, an integration scope and requirements document, technical recommendations with the tradeoffs made explicit, and an implementation plan you could hand to any team, including ours.

Implementation: we build and deliver the solution

The plan becomes the thing that actually runs. We build the integrations, automate the workflows around them, and don't call it done until the data holds up under real operating conditions, not just a demo environment.

  • Build integrations using APIs, iPaaS platforms and custom code
  • Configure systems and automate the workflows around them
  • Test until the data is something you'd trust with a live customer order or shipment
  • Deploy, monitor, and tune for performance under real load
  • Stay on for support as the system beds into daily use
Walk away with: systems that are genuinely connected, workflows that don't need someone babysitting them, measurable operational efficiency, and people who are actually using what you built.
